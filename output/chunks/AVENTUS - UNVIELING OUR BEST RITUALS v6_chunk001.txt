HUDDLE 8:50AM

At 8.50 every morning, business units join

the company wide huddle, in which

team shares;

 1 highlight (from yesterday)

 1 roadblock

 or 1 key learning

 1 focus (for the day)

 Work out updates and key metrics

 Plus key announcements – birthdays, engagements

and team successes……

QUARTERLY TEAM FORUMS

WHAT?

2 day team workshop every quarter

COST?

An investment of $25,000 every quarter

WHY?

 Drives culture

 Share learnings & wins

 Training

 Public praise and reward

 Team unity and fun

 Communicate our vision

 Share our goal progress

 Practice presentations

GOAL SETTING

WHAT?

Our blue print for our year ahead & we have been doing it successfully for 13 years

COST?

No external cost, however it requires creativity, forward thinking and 2 days per year

of preparing and reviewing goals

WHY?

 Creates individual and peer accountability

 Drives high performance

 Encourages creativity, risk taking and stretch goals

 Enhances our team’s competitive spirit

 Alignment to bonuses and financial outcomes

 Celebrate successes and wins

 Full quarterly review

 Power of public declaration
