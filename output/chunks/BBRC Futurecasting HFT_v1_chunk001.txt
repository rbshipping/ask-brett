BB Intro – what are we talking about today?

<number>

This is one hour for you to step out of “everyday” and think big (part of your role as a CEO)

‘Her’ expectations have never been higher… our customers are very informed

… but what is now possible, is also much greater (Moore’s Law)

This is broad, across a number of different areas from customer facing to purely operational

Much more than just online shopping, or being ‘multi-channel’

I certainly don’t have the answers to how we use technology to our advantage… but we need to determine what’s possible, what we should do and when

“Retailers relying on earlier formats either adapt or die out as the new ones pull volume from their stores and make the remaining volume less profitable.” – Harvard Business Review, 2011

I think we all know this… but what are we doing about

it?
