The Plane Game – Rules

Objective: Create as many paper airplanes as possible

within 30 seconds.

Criteria for Success: Plane must be able to fly 2 meters to

be deemed successful.

Manufacturing Assumptions:

- Labour … $15.00 for 30 seconds

- Materials … $0.50 per airplane

(whether successful or not)

BBRC WORLDWIDE

The Plane Game

- Break into groups of 6 where you are sitting

- When timer starts, build your planes

- Timer stops, stop building (all hands in the air)

- We will fly them by team

- Count planes built / successful flight

- Review results

- Repeat exercise
