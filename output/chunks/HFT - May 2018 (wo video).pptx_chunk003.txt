▪ Alternative to E-tivity

▪ Plug in to World Manager - 6 week implementation process

▪ Benefits:

• Appears as a module on World Manager (Team already familiar with World Manager platform)

• Ability to have schedule as a widget on the matrix home page

• Mobile friendly – rosters can be prepared on tablet

• Team can have app downloaded and view rosters, will get push notifications of shift approaching

and notes to say “missed a shift” – this app has capacity for Team to accept shifts

• Area Managers no longer have to use employee registration to set Team up – reduce admin tasks

with one less platform to set them up on

• If a Team Member isn’t clocked on for their start time, the Area Manager receives notification

• Strong data analysis capacity and ability to adjust wage costs based on variances: sales, unit

transactions, customer count, trends

• Monitor % to sales

• Charts and graphs to show forecasting peaks – provides control over increments of time
