BBRC Culture Day – Overview / Purpose

• A Day that Recognizes, Rewards, and Celebrates BBRC Culture.

• Refreshes, revs up, teaches / explains the why + purpose, and sends

everyone back to promote & demonstrate culture.

• Culture is all about sharing values, vision, purpose, expectations,

behaviours, views … what better way to share, than have a day specifically

focused on that?

• Celebrated & Recognized across all the businesses

o 3% of all global employees get to attend, ~100+ people

o All full time team members selected by their peers … not senior

management

o All new or promoted “executives” within the business are required to attend

BBRC Culture Day – Selection Process

• Selected from & by the Global Team

• Single query amongst global team

o “Who do you think best represents the culture?”

• Key Points

o Need to drive response rate within business, need to have coordinated communications

o Responses … anticipating a high concentration for a select few, and then a long tail. Those

within the long tail will be chosen at random above a certain cut-off (ie 2 or 3 name

submissions).

• Survey sent to every team member

• Timeline

o April – Survey sent to all members

o May – Responses received

o June – Participants selected & announced

o August 14th (Wednesday – day before BBRC University) … BBRC Culture Day
