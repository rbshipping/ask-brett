It is important to recognise that our Team Members learn both for informal and formal

processes. No matter how it takes place knowledge is power but it is crucial to recognise that

informal learning makes up 80% of the learning in most businesses.

Formal processes do play an important part in any business as it often establishes a solid

foundation for things such as compliance, policy and procedure and as such should not be

neglected. Informal processes on the other hand are vital as they takes place on a daily basis.

People learn to do their job through informal learning, not in a classroom setting.
