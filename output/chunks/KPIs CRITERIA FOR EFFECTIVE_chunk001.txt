CRITERIA FOR EFFECTIVE

PERFORMANCE INDICTATORS

1. Is it measurable?

2. Is the measure important?

3. Is the measure feasible to track?

4. Is the data available at least every 2 weeks?

5. Will the performer understand the measure?

6. Does the performer influence the measure?

7. Is the measure stated in positive terms?

What I learned at Wal-Mart

I was reminded that measuring everything is the

easiest part, actually holding people

accountable to doing something with that

measurement to ensure that the business

improves is the key to measuring, the only

reason anything should be measured.
