Cultivate your Culture — Essential for motivation

e

Have culture of customer service

Not created overnight

Don’t try and fool staff

Culture is about what you do and how people feel about it. At Richer combination of

incentives, gestures, thank you's ~ not isolated issues.

Incentives not used add to culture as staff know they are available

Hard work, integrity, honesty also part

People make the culture the culture is the people — the 2 things are interchangeable

Staff feed the culture through suggestions, attitude etc

However, in the first place culture created by people who run an organisation — about

place of business in wider society

Position explained in catalogue

Giving better value than competition also part

Environmental awareness commercial reality — be friendly when possible

Mission statement essential — but not culture
