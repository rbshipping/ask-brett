A Great Store Visit

A Great Store Visit

Tip: Don’t make visual merchandising and what

you can see, the first thing you focus on or take

action on.

What you see in the split second before the

team notice you is what is really happening, the

real behaviour and should be an influencing

part of your assessment and plan

A Great Store Visit

• Be prepared:

– Numbers

– KPIs

– General performance and attitude

• Know who is rostered on and what their skills

are

– Check their knowledge

– Test different things i.e. click clack, refund policy, what do

you do when…

A Great Store Visit

• Presentation

• Attitude

• Diary/In The Pink/IQ

– A store manager can do a good job just by getting these

three right

• Is the store loved?

- Clean

- Up to date

A Great Store Visit

• Determine what is great/not so great

– Positive discussion and correction

• Take action

– If you see wrong, ‘right’ it

– If you see right – appreciate it

• Produce actions

– To do

– Plans

– Follow up

• Find ways to make your team’s job easier

A Great Store Visit

• A little leading by example is always good

• Help/support/teach/lead

Christmas Tips

Christmas Tips

• You have what you have

– Lead and motivate

– Stay calm, positive, supportive and enthusiastic

• Deal with the bigger issues in January (Don’t

take this literally)

• Be ready to flex up and down

Christmas Tips

• Christmas is busy

– Everyone must move faster (a little or a lot)

– A leader must set the pace – up the tempo, work

hard and keep moving

• Mop the floor/empty the bin (occasionally)

• Think 5 January – not 25 December

• Audit the markdowns and sale product

– No missed markdowns

We are human

• Touch your team everyday

• We are all human and need to be emotionally

connected

• Remember to communicate the

positives/wins

impact on their

people than they

think they do
